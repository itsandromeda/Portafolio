Hello! I'm Annia, a multidisciplinary designer based in Lima, Peru who focuses on UX/UI Design.

My UX/UI journey started in 2017 after I finished a Web Development program at Laboratoria. I took Visual Design & User Experience courses after the bootcamp because I wanted to focus on my visual approach to problem solving and my deep interest in people.

I'm looking to become a part of a team of entrepreneurs and visionaries determined to change the world with design and technology. I believe I would fit in perfectly in a design-driven team that works with agile methodologies, since I've been trained for it.

Interests

I'm really interested in Visual Design, UX Research, social issues and entrepreneurship.
